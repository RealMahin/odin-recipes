Strawberry-Rhubarb Hand Pies

Description

With the perfect balance of sweet and tart. These hand pies are one of my personal favorites.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup finely diced frest strawberries
  • 3/4 cup finely diced rhubarb
  • 1 Tbls cornstarch
  • 6 Tbls sugar, divided
  • 3 tsp orange zest, divided
  • 2 1/4 cups AP flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup shortening, chilled
  • 3 Tbls ice cold water
  • 3 Tbls orange juice
  • 1 egg yolk, beaten
  • 1 Tbsp whipped cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 Fahrenheit
  2. Combine strawberries, rhubarb, cornstarch, 2 Tbls sugar and 1/2 tsp orange zest in a small bowl; Stir and set aside.
  3. In a seperate large bowl combine flour, salt, and remaining 1/4 cup sugar.
  4. Cut in butter and shortening with a pastry blender until dough resembles small peas; Drizzle with ice cold water and orange juice.
  5. Stir with a fork until combined. (Dough will be dry and crumbly)
  6. Kneed lightly, and shape dough into a disk; Divide dough in half.
  7. Roll half of dough to 1/8-inch thickness on a heavily floured surface.
  8. Cut with a 2 1/4-inch round cutter, reroll scraps as needed.
  9. Place half of those dough rounds 2 inches apart on a parchment paper-lined baking sheets.
  10. Top each of the rounds with 1 heaping tsp mixture.
  11. Dampen edge of dough with water and top with remaining dough rounds; press edges lightly with finger.
  12. Crimp edges with a fork, and cut a slit on top of each round for steam to escape.
  13. Repeat steps 7-12 with remaining dough and strawberry mixture.
  14. Stir together egg yolk and whipped cream; brush pies with egg wash and sprinkle with sugar.
  15. Freeze pies for 10 minutes.
  16. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until lightly browned.
  17. Cool for 10 minutes.
