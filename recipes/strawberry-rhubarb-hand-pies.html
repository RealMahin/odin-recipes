Strawberry-Rhubarb Hand Pies

Description

With the perfect balance of sweet and tart. These hand pies are one of my personal favorites.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup finely diced frest strawberries
  • 3/4 cup finely diced rhubarb
  • 1 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 6 Tbsp sugar, divided
  • 3 tsp orange zest, divided
  • 2 1/4 cups AP flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup shortening, chilled
  • 3 Tbsp ice cold water
  • 3 Tbsp orange juice
  • 1 egg yolk, beaten
  • 1 Tbsp whipped cream
