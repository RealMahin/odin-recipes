Ham and Swiss Braid

Description

A meal that looks as good as it tastes.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups AP flour
  • 2 Tbls sugar
  • 2 packages(1/4 oz each) quick rise yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup dijon mustard
  • 2 Tbls butter
  • 1 lb thinly sliced deli ham
  • 1 cup shredded swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped dill pickles
  • 1 egg lightly beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 Fahrenheit.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine 3 cups AP flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  3. In a small sauce pan heat water, dijon mustard, and butter to 120-130 Fahrenheit.
  4. Add flour; Stir in enough remaining flour to form a soft dough (dough will be stiff).
  5. Turn onto a lightly floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, about 6 to 8 minutes.
  6. Roll dough into a 14 in. X 12 in. rectangle, on a greased baking sheet.
  7. Arrange half of the ham over the dough; Top with cheese, pickles and remaining ham.
  8. On each long side, cut 3/4 inch wide strips about 2-1/2 in. into the center.
  9. Starting at one end, fold alternating strips at an angle across filling; Pinch ends to seal.
  10. Cover and let rise for 15 minutes; Brush with beaten egg.
  11. Bake at 375 Fahrenheit for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown; Serve warm.
